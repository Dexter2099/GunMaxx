# GunMaxx — One Pager

## Project identity

GunMaxx is a **top-down room-based action shooter**.

- **Run structure:** inspired by **Enter the Gungeon** (room-to-room runs, encounter sequence, pickups between fights).
- **Combat pressure:** inspired by **Hotline Miami** (high lethality, fast decisions, constant threat).
- **Combat target:** fast, dodge-heavy, and readable.

## Design pillars

1. **Readable pressure** — Enemy intent, bullet threat, and safe movement options are legible in motion.
2. **Room-first progression** — Clear a room, take a short reward beat, move to the next encounter.
3. **Geometry matters** — Obstacles and cover shape routes, line of sight, and target priority.
4. **Constrained MVP scope** — Prove the core loop before adding campaign breadth or deep meta systems.

## Hard constraints

- **Offline only** — No network dependency for play, saves, or progression.
- **Single-player only** — No multiplayer or social features in MVP.
- **Solo-first scope** — Keep systems small enough to ship and tune.
